# Cisco Virtual Images – Quick Reference

| Image                      | OS family                                | Primary use                    | Key capabilities                                                                                | Major limitations                                                          | Typical vCPU / RAM                        | Licensing / notes                   |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Dynamips (classic IOS)** | IOS 12.x/15.x (1700/2600/3600/3700/7200) | Legacy routing labs            | OSPF/EIGRP/BGP; basic L2 via NM-16ESW; some MPLS on 7200                                        | Old platforms; no IOS-XE; limited modern QoS/crypto; rudimentary switching | varies (e.g., 3725 ≈256 MB; 7200 ≈512 MB) | Needs physical IOS images; emulator |
| **IOL/IOU-L3**             | IOS on Linux/Unix (internal)             | Lightweight routing            | Fast boot, small footprint; broad IOS features                                                  | Internal-only; behavior quirks; licensing murky                            | ~0–1 vCPU / ~1 GB                         | Unofficial outside Cisco            |
| **IOL/IOU-L2**             | IOS on Linux/Unix (internal)             | Lightweight switching          | VLAN/Trunk, STP (PVST/RPVST/MST), EtherChannel                                                  | Quirks vs Catalyst; internal-only                                          | ~0–1 vCPU / ~1 GB                         | Same licensing caveat               |
| **IOSv (router)**          | Virtual IOS                              | Core routing practice          | OSPF/EIGRP/BGP, VRF-lite, NAT/ACL/ZBF, IPv6, DMVPN/IPsec (CPU-bound)                            | **No MPLS**; software dataplane                                            | ~1 vCPU / 512 MB                          | Ships with CML; QEMU/KVM            |
| **IOSvL2 (switch)**        | Virtual IOS L2                           | Switching fundamentals         | VLAN/Trunk, PVST/RPVST/MST, EtherChannel, SVIs/HSRP, SPAN, port-security                        | No ASIC fidelity; no StackWise/VSS; QoS limited                            | ~1 vCPU / 768 MB                          | Ships with CML; QEMU/KVM            |
| **CSR1000v (IOS-XE)**      | IOS-XE                                   | Modern routing/edge            | BGP/OSPF/EIGRP, VRF, IPsec/DMVPN, **NETCONF/RESTCONF/YANG**, (MPLS/L3VPN on supported releases) | Heavier; feature licensing needed                                          | ~1 vCPU / **4 GB**                        | Trial/throughput licenses           |
| **Catalyst 8000V**         | IOS-XE (Cat8k)                           | Edge/SD-WAN & XE               | Routing, IPsec/SD-WAN, model-driven telemetry                                                   | Not a switch; heavier than IOSv                                            | ~1 vCPU / **4 GB**                        | Throughput-tier licenses            |
| **Catalyst 9000v**         | IOS-XE (Cat9k switch)                    | Modern Catalyst switching feel | L2/L3, XE CLI; integrates with Catalyst Center concepts                                         | Resource-heavy; beta-ish; software dataplane only                          | ~**4 vCPU / 18 GB**                       | Included in newer CML refplats      |
| **NX-OSv 9000**            | NX-OS                                    | DC/Nexus CLI                   | VLAN; some VXLAN; routing                                                                       | Limited/perf-capped; DC-focused                                            | ~2 vCPU / 12 GB                           | For Nexus practice                  |
| **ASAv**                   | ASA                                      | Firewall/VPN                   | NAT, ACL, IPsec/RA VPN (license-dependent)                                                      | Throughput tied to license; no hardware offload                            | ~1 vCPU / 2–4 GB                          | License tiers map to throughput     |
| **IOS XRv 9000**           | IOS XR                                   | Service Provider               | IS-IS/OSPF/BGP, SR/MPLS (lab)                                                                   | Heavy; SP-specific                                                         | ~4 vCPU / 16–20 GB                        | CML ref platform                    |
